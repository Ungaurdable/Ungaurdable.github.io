CK Academy

Origininally CK Academy just started because during COVID me and my friends who played basketball needed somewhere to play. We started practicing a lot and playing a lot, but we wanted to play as a team. We got Coach Lamar to coach us for a long time through COVID and he helped us get so much better.

Now, we go out and play in some AAU tournaments, not in any big-time circuits, but just some tournaments. Coach Cyrus coaches us and some of us are playing for our school team. I'm also coaching a grassroots team and they are playing in U9 tournaments.
